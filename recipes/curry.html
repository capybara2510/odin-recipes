Japanese Curry

Description

Delicious Japanese chicken curry recipe for a weeknight dinner! Tender pieces of chicken, carrots, and potatoes cooked in a rich savory curry sauce, this Japanese version of curry is a must-have for your family meal.

Ingredients (4 Servings)

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, (cut into thick wedges)
  • 2 cloves garlic, (minced)
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, (cut into bite sized pieces)
  • 2 carrots, (cut into wedges)
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 apple, (peeled and grated)
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 teaspoons Kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
  • 2 potatoes, (cut into 1.5 inch chunks and soak for 15min.)
  • 1 packet S&B Golden Curry Sauce Mix, (Medium Hot)

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat and saut√© the onions until they become translucent.

  2. Add the garlic, cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.

  3. Add the chicken and cook until the chicken is opaque.

  4. Stir in the carrots and chicken broth.

  5. Bring the broth to boil and skim the scrum and fat from the surface of the chicken broth.

  6. Add grated apple, honey, salt, and pepper. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  7. Add thr potatoes and cook for 15 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender, and turn off the heat.

  8. Add the curry, 1-2 blocks at a time, in a ladle and slowly let it dissolve with spoon until all incorporated. STIR!

  9. Simmer uncovered on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the curry becomes thick.

  10. Serve the curry the choice of your rice. ENJOY!
